Piano Sonata No.1

Piano Sonata No. 1 is a simple chord progression utilizing Steinberg's Halion Yamaha Keyboard VST. This is my first piano sonata. I'm not a pianist and have only just started understanding the different articulations that a pianist utilizes. It was a nice short exercise for one who is accustomed to stringed instruments.

Vanishing

Vanishing is a light pop song that incorporates a drum kit type sound along with an acoustic rhythm guitar. It was first recorded with just my acoustic guitar on a four track digital recorder.

R&D

This song was originally titled 'Blackwidow' (named in 2007) and was about a female character who loved to misbehave, however it morphed into a much more mad scientist type of song. More of a feeling of trying to find the answer towards a deep mystery. This started as just pop rock with only dirty guitars, but enventually I started adding strings to it as I went. I'm going to eventually lower the arpeggiated nature of it at a later time.
